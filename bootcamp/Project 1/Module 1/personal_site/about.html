Here's what I am up to when I am off the clock!

Reading

I have been an avid reader since I was a tiny child! I enjoy reading a variety of topics across a variety of mediums, but I primarily enjoy reading books (both fiction and non-fiction) as well as journalist articles. Despite my aforementioned enjoyment of reading since my youth, I lack a clean and tidy list of all-time favorites, but some books I have enjoyed recently include: A Little Life by Hanya Yanagihara, This Chair Rocks by Ashton Applewhite, and Normal People by Sally Rooney.

I am currently looking for any surreal recommendations, so if you have any favorites, I would love to hear them!

Hiking

Growing up near the Chattahoochee River, I did not have to venture far from my house to find some lovely trails to hike on. I have always enjoyed going on walks and hikes, and I currently have the great fortune of living near a plethora of trails! My partner and I are currently making our way down a list of various State and National trails nearby, and our favorites so far include the Sweetwater Creek State Park and the Kennesaw Mountain Trails.

Gaming

Similar to my other hobbies, I started playing video games from a young age and have not stopped yet! Although I love video games themselves, my favorite part of the hobby is meeting and befriending other people. That enjoyment of meeting others is largely the reason that I used to stream video games on Twitch.tv a few years back!

These days, I find myself replaying old favorites like Resident Evil 5, Deadly Premonition, or Mario Party 5, but I have played and enjoyed games from almost every genre (aside from Dancing games due to my tragic abilities at dance), and I am always excited to have a friendly discussion about anything video games related!
